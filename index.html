Instructions

Welcome to TIS's CUP and Equiment order form. Please follow the instructions below:

  1. Fill out your user and departmental information to the left.
  2. Choose between Dell, Apple, Levnovo, or tablets from the top.
  3. Select your preconfigured builds below.
  4. If there is a component that you wanted to change within your preconfigured build, then plesae make the appropriate selections under "Preset Customizations".
  5. If you will need any software purchased with your new computer setup make the appropriate selections under "Additional Software".
  6. If there is an option missing, or if you have any special instructions that you would like applied to your setup, please add those comments in the field below.
  7. Final, click submit, and this will create an email with all your selections. Please review, then send.
